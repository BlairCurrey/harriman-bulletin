The Park

Harriman is a 47,527 acre New York state park. It is New Yorks 2nd largest state park and is only 30 miles north of New York City. It boasts 200 miles of hiking trails, 31 lakes, two beaches, abundant wildlife, scenic drives, multiple camping options, and lots of history. The land was donated by the Harriman estate and the State of New York has encorporated additional parcels over the years. As a result, you can find ruinous developments (such as housing foundations and abandoned mines) buried in the woods.
